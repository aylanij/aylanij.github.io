Our Story

We met the modern way: by swiping right on a dating app (swoon!) in 2021. Couple days later we met for the first time and hit it off right away. Never ending conversations, laughters, and glazed pizza (Karan's favorite!). It really only took that one date to know that we had something special. …. After two years of endless dates, beautiful moments, video calls, snow, rain, and months of staying apart we’re each others’ best friends and so in love! And we cannot wait to spend the rest of our lives together.
